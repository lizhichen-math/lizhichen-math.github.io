  1. Week of February 16, 2025

    February 25: Interior and closure of a subset

  3. Week of March 02, 2025

    March 04: Convergence of a sequence, Hausdorff space

    March 13: Construct new spaces from the old (1): subspace, product space

  5. Week of March 16, 2025

    March 18: Construct new spaces from the old (2): quotient space

  6. Week of March 23, 2025

    March 25: Construct new spaces from the old (4): topological groups

    March 27: Connectedness and path-connectedness (1)

  7. Week of March 30, 2025
