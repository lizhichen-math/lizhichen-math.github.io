Geometry and Topology Student Seminar


This is a geometry and topology seminar for graduate student or senior undergraduate student.

Talks are conducted either in person (with hybrid options) or online, as indicated. In-person sessions are usually scheduled on Friday, while online talks are scheduled in China Standard Time (GMT+8). Usually talks are recorded. Videos will be made available online upon approval of the speakers. If you are interested in presenting a talk, please send a message to chenlzh@lzu.edu.cn.

If you wish to be on the seminar mailing list and receive announcements, please send an email to chenlzh@lzu.edu.cn. Or if you wish to be removed from the mailing list, please send an email to the same address.
